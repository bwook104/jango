Prediction of pressure loss coefficient of 90°bends

User guide

This program offers a pressure loss coefficient of 90°bends for designing engineering system.

Attention: be award of units before using this program.

How to use

1. Enter the geometrical conditions of the 90°bend. (Pipe diameter and curvature radius)

2. Enter the properties of the fluid. (Density and dynamic viscosity)

3. Enter the working condition of the system. (Volume flow rate)

4. Press the prediction button to get a pressure loss coefficient.
